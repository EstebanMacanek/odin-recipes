Oven Baked Omelet

Description

This oven-baked omelet is the perfect recipe for a crowd. The oven helps the eggs stay fluffy and yummy.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Soup cream
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish with butter.
  2. Beat eggs, sour cream, milk, and salt in a bowl until blended. Stir in green onions. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until set, 25 to 30 minutes. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese over eggs and continue baking until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes more.
